Ulysses by Alfred Lord Tennyson:

Come my friends, '

This not too late to seek a newer world for my purpose holds to sail beyond the sunset.

And though we are not now that strength which in old days Moved earth and heaven;

that which we are, we are;--

One equal temper of heroic hearts, Made weak by time and fate,

but strong in will. To strive, to seek, to find, and not to yield.

丁尼生「尤利西斯」：

來吧，我的朋友，尋找更新世界尚為時不晚。

我決心已定，要駛過夕陽盡頭，

儘管我們不再有昔日的力量，可以震天撼地，

我們仍有著，同樣的雄心壯志。

時間和命運，使它衰老，

但堅強意志仍在，讓我們去奮鬥，去探索，去發現。
